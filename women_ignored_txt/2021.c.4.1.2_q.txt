In 1903, President Theodore Roosevelt set aside this island's Crown Lands of El Yunque  to establish a forest reserve, and today, that region of this island is the only tropical U.S National Forest. The Arecibo  Observatory, a radio telescope on this island, collapsed in late 2020. This island's capital city of San Juan is the oldest European settlement in what is now the United States.
