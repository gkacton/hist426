Yigal Yadin excavated this site, which can be approached via the "Snake Path". Josephus recorded a speech by this site's defender in which he ordered that a banquet be set. This site, which new recruits into the Israeli army vow “shall not fall again” was besieged by the Tenth Legion and it was the last holdout of the Zealots. For 10 points, name this giant rock mesa on which Eliezer ben Jair's followers supposedly committed suicide rather than be captured by the Romans.
