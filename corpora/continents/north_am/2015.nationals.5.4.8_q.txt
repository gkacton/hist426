A proposed extension of this system in the Vieux Carre  area was the subject of a controversy called the "Second Battle of New Orleans." The financial viability of this system was assessed by the Clay Committee. This system was planned in the Yellow Book, and it realized the goals proposed in the Pershing Map. Lady Bird Johnson campaigned for a  "Beautification Act" affecting this system. A 1919 army convoy trip and the  Autobahn inspired President Eisenhower to champion the creation of this system.
