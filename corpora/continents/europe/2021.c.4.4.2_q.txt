In the prelude to this battle, one side scored easy victories at Bordeaux  and Narbonne over Duke Odo the Great. Prior to this battle, Pope Gregory III provided a loan to a "Mayor of the Palace" nicknamed "The Hammer" to repel an Umayyad  invasion of Gaul.
