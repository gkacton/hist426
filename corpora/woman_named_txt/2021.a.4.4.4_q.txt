In Medieval England, the eggs of this specific animal were fed to children to ward off drunkenness and as a cure to blindness. One of these animals named Ko'Ko, or the "Watcher of the dark," is the underworld deity of the Hopi. A contingent of this specific bird flying over the battle site of  Salamis signaled a Greek victory over the Persians according to Plutarch. The Athenian tetradrachma depicts this  bird and symbol of the city on its backside. For ten points, name this bird which symbolized wisdom in Ancient Greece and is associated with Athena.
