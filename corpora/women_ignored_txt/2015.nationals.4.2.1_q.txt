The last person to take a photo of this object in its entirety was Jack Schmidtt, who was accompanied by Eugene Cernan and Ronald Evans. Carl Sagan titled the sequel to Cosmos for a photograph of this object. Another photograph taken by Bill Anders of this object is titled for its "rise," and was taken during the first manned mission to orbit the moon. For 10 points, name this object photographed by Voyager 1 in "Pale Blue Dot" and by Apollo 17 in "The Blue Marble."
