This man is the namesake of the smallest and lightest nuclear weapons unit designed by the United States. After getting his start in the Lawrence County militia, this man was elected to the Tennessee General Assembly and then to the House of Representatives, where he opposed Indian Removal. With James Bowie and William Travis, he died as a volunteer at the Alamo. For 10 points, name this American known as the “King of the Wild Frontier,” who wore a coonskin cap.
