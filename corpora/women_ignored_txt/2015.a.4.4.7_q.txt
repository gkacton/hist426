One ruler from this kingdom led the “Long campaign” and was captured following a defeat at the battle of Varna. The Black Army led by the son of that ruler successfully besieged Vienna and engaged in many conflicts with the Ottomans. Besides  Janos Hunyadi  and Matthias Corvinus , other leaders from the Arpad dynasty here include its founder,  Stephen I. The Treaty of Trianon formally abolished this kingdom, whose privileges were confirmed by the Ausgleich  of 1867.
