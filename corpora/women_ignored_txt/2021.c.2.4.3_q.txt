This city was founded in 1565 by Pedro Menéndez de Avilés  on the feast day of the patron of brewers. Henry Flagler built a railroad through this city, which was annexed into the U.S. along with the rest of its state in the Adams-Onís Treaty. This city was intended to be turned into a winter resort by an industrialist who constructed the Hotel Alcazar and Hotel Ponce de Leon.  For ten points, name this Florida city, the oldest continuously-inhabited European settlement in the United States.
