Although this river's source is only 150 miles from shore, it unusually runs for over seventeen times that length. The Jebba and Kainji Dams produce power from this river. Its oil-rich delta is home to cities such as  Port Harcourt. The Mandinka people live in this river's basin, which was explored by Mungo Park.
