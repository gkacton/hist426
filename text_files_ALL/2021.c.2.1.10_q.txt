In Islam, this book is identified with a pre-Islamic holy book titled Zabur. Portions of this book labelled mizmor were originally intended to be sung. Many sections of this book are attributed to Asaph and the Sons of Korah. The 23rd chapter of this book begins with the line "The Lord is my shepherd, I shall not want."
