Several city-states of this civilization were defeated by their rival, Spearthrower Owl, a leader of Teotihuacan [[teh-oh-tee-HWAH-kan]]. The military outpost of Dos Pilas [[PEE-lahss]] switched between this civilization's two rival cities of Calakmul [[kah-lahk-MOOL]] and Tikal [[tee-KAHL]]. The Temple of Kukulcan [[koo-kool-KAHN]] was built in this civilization's settlement of Chichen Itza [[CHEE-chen EE-tzah]].
