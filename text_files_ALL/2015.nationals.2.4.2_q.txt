The Scottish urban planner Patrick Geddes created a 1925 "plan" for this city's layout. This city shares its name with a translation of the political novel Altneuland. One area of this city postdates but now contains the residential area (+) Neve Tzedek. This city is home to the Habima theater and a city center around Rothschild Avenue. The growth of this city, whose name literally means (*) "spring hill," caused the much older nearby city of Jaffa to dwindle until this city absorbed it.
