A fixture on the Rouen [[roo-EHN]] Cathedral is known as the "[This Commodity] Tower" due to having been built by churchgoers who gave up this item up for Lent. Propagandist Joseph Goebbels stated, "One cannot shoot with [this commodity], but with guns" in a speech justifying peacetime rationing. A challenge by Napoleon III to invent a substitute for this foodstuff for the poor led to the invention of margarine. Described by Pliny as "the most delicate of food among barbarous nations," for ten points, what is this dairy product made by churning?
