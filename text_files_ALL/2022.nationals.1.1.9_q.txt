In this kingdom, Savoyard mason Master James of Saint George built a ring of fortifications, including Beaumaris [[boh-mah-REH]], following the passage of the Statute of Rhuddlan. Rhys Gethin [[REES GEH-tihn]] and a national hero of this kingdom, Owain Glendower, defeated the forces of Henry IV at the Battle of Stalling Down. A ruler of this region's kingdom of Powys [[POW-iss]], Llywelyn [[loo-ELL-in]] the Last, swore fealty to Edward I following the English conquest of this modern country.
