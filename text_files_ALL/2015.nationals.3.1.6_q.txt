This ruler signed the Peace of Amasya with Tahmasp I, giving him control of Baghdad. This leader was known to his subjects as Kanuni or “Lawgiver”. He ordered the death of his oldest son Mustafa due to the influence of his wife Hürrem Sultan and executed grand vizier Ibrahim Pasha. His admiral Hayreddin Barbarossa won the Battle of Preveza and he defeated Louis II of Hungary at the Battle of Mohács (pr. MOE-hotch). For 10 points, name this longest reigning Ottoman sultan.
