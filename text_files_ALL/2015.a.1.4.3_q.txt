A depiction of this place in the parekklesion [pare-uh-KLEE-see-onn] of the Chora [CHOH-ruh] Church features Jesus holding the hands of Adam and Eve. This place is commonly depicted as a "mouth" in manuscript paintings, which often feature depictions of its [+] “harrowing.” The New Testament repeatedly refers to its inhabitants “weeping and gnashing” their (*) teeth. This place is depicted in the right-hand panel of Bosch’s Garden of Earthly Delights and is usually on the right in paintings of the Last Judgement.
