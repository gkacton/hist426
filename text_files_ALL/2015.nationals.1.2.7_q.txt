The language of Ilokano is widely spoken only on this island. This island’s Lingayen Gulf was the launching point for a World War II invasion of it. The Bessang Pass and Balete Pass were pitched World War II battles on this island. Daniel Burnham designed this island’s city of Baguio after the Spanish conceded it and its southern partners Palawan and Mindanao to the US after the Spanish American War.
