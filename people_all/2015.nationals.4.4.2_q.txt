This man claimed an enemy moved "with the perseverance of a mighty avalanche." This man was defended by Francis Scott Key after he assaulted Congressman William Stanberry. He suffered a bullet wound in his ankle prior to the signing of the Treaty of   Velasco. The only man to be elected governor of two different U.S. states, he was also the only Southern governor to oppose  secession. He was succeeded as President by Mirabeau Lamar.
